Travel and mental health

A free website about travelling for people with mental health problems.

Welcome to Travel Mental Health.

This website makes it easy for people to find and access various mental health resources and find advice regarding traveling aborad with a mental illness. Anyone can contribute to this site and add suggestions that imrpove it and make it better for everyone gh-pages at this location. From design the code, there are limitless contributions that anyone can make to helping people with mental illness navigate and thrive as they travel the world.
